The fast-growing sport of stand up paddle boarding (sup) is an excellent, easily accessible work-out and a fun way to explore the Dutch canals and rivers. Free Movement sup en kayak huren Haarlem offers fun sup tours in Amsterdam and Haarlem, helps you to learn the basics of supping or provides expert workshops to sharpen your skills.

Please bring a towel and clothing for swimming (bathing suit on warm days, for colder days running clothes work well.)
