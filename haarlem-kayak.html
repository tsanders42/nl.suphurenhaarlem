Rent a kayak to explore Haarlem in a fun an relaxed way. Because kayaking is a low impact activity, it offers and accessible way to exercise and fun way to explore the waterways. We have single seat and double seat kayaks available.

Your kayak adventure starts in the city centre of Haarlem on the river Spaarne.

How to prepare for your kayak trip?
We recommend you wear sport clothing. Water drops will fall from your paddle, so you should expect to get a little wet. Quick drying sport clothes are therefore ideal. You can wear sneakers, or go barefoot.
