Who we are

Free movement sup en kayak huren Haarlem is a company based in Haarlem, delivering quality services in outdoor sport activities, Stand Up Paddle (SUP), Biking and Hiking. We operate mostly in and arond Haarlem and Amsterdam. We provide our customers the equipment and the expertise of professional instructors and athletes.

Free movement sup en kayak huren Haarlem is managed by Claudio Duran. Claudio is a SUP athlete, who has competed 6 years in different locations worldwide and participated in the 2019 and 2020 edition of SUP 11 SUP City Tour. Since this year, he is Mistral team rider competing in the 2021 European tour and the Dutch national championships.
